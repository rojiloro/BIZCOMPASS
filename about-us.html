What Do We Do?

Nurtured from the seed of a single great idea - to empower the traveller - BizCompass was formed. Our aim is to revolutionise the business travel industry over the coming years. This is BizCompass, India’s First Exclusive Business Travel Partner.

Remaining reliable, efficient and at the forefront of technology, BizCompass’ commitment and customer-centricity allows it to better understand and provide for its customers’ diverse needs and wants, and deliver consistently. With dedicated 24x7 customer support, BizCompass is there for you, whenever and wherever.

Unlike the ubiqutious websites avaible online, we are one of a kind as we are dedicated to the needs of a business traveller. BizCompass is much more than just a travel portal - it is a one-stop-travel-shop that offers the broadest selection of travel products and services in India, including expense management, itinerary preparation and much more. Our aim is to take the work load off of the business traveller and make him as comfortable as possible.
